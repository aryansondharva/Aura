# ✨ Aura - Simple Summary

**Aura** is a powerful yet easy-to-use study app that turns your notes and books into your own personal interactive tutor.

---

## 🎯 Our Goal
To help you study smarter, not harder. We use smart tools to read your books for you so you can focus on learning and remembering what matters.

---

## 💎 Why You'll Love Aura

- **Your Private Tutor**: Talk to any PDF or book and get answers in seconds.
- **Built-in Memory Magic**: Aura reminds you when to study so you don't forget.
- **Super Simple Design**: It looks beautiful and is very easy to use on any device.
- **Fully Hands-Free**: You can use your voice to control the whole app.
