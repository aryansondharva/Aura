# Aura Voice Assistant Commands 🎙️

Welcome to the **Aura Voice Documentation**. The Murf AI Voice Assistant provides a seamless, hands-free way to navigate the platform, control your screen, and check on your progress. 

To use these commands, simply ensure the microphone is active (the orb in the bottom right corner will be yellow and pulsing). It will continuously listen for these keywords in your sentences.

---

### 🗺️ Full Navigation Commands
Say any of these phrases to instantly jump to specific parts of the app:
*   **"Home"** or **"Go home"** - Takes you to the landing page.
*   **"Dashboard"** - Opens your main dashboard.
*   **"Chat"** - Opens a new AI chat session.
*   **"Feature"** or **"Features"** - Navigates to the Features page.
*   **"Developer"** - Takes you to the Developers/Team page.
*   **"Todo"** or **"Task"** - Opens your task list.
*   **"Settings"** - Opens the settings page.
*   **"Profile"** - Views your user profile.
*   **"Login"** or **"Sign in"** - Takes you to the sign-in page.
*   **"Topic"** or **"Subject"** - Opens the study topics view.
*   **"Exam"**, **"Test"**, or **"Quiz"** - Navigates straight to the exam arena.
*   **"Progress"** or **"Performance"** - Opens your progress and score report.
*   **"Archive"** or **"History"** - Accesses your past exam archives.
*   **"Metrics"** or **"Analytics"** - Takes you to your detailed study analytics.

---

### 🖱️ Screen Control Commands
Control your browser layout completely hands-free:
*   **"Scroll down"** - Smoothly scrolls you down the current page.
*   **"Scroll up"** - Smoothly scrolls you up the current page.
*   **"Scroll to top"** or **"Go to top"** - Instantly jumps to the very top of the page.
*   **"Scroll to bottom"** or **"Go to bottom"** - Instantly jumps to the very bottom of the page.
*   **"Go back"** - Returns to the previous page you were on.
*   **"Reload"** or **"Refresh"** - Refreshes the browser page.

---

### 🤖 Conversational & Extra Commands
Interact with your personal AI:
*   **"Logout"** or **"Sign out"** - Safely logs you out and returns to the home screen.
*   **"What time is it"** or **"Current time"** - Aura will read you the current local time.
*   **"Tell a joke"** - Aura will read you a developer joke.
*   **"Who are you"** or **"What are you"** - Aura will introduce herself.
*   **"Thank you"** or **"Thanks"** - Acknowledgement response.
*   **"Hello"**, **"Hi Aura"**, or **"Are you there"** - A friendly greeting string to initialize Aura.

---

### 💡 Pro-Tip
The voice recognition system uses **keyword detecting**. You do not have to speak like a robot! 
You can use natural language, such as:
> *"Hey Aura, I'm feeling ready, let's take an **exam**."*
> *"Can you please take me back to my **dashboard**?"*

As long as the bolded keyword is detected, Aura will execute the action perfectly mapping it with Murf AI's premium voice playback.
